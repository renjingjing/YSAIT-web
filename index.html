Innovation and Technology

超越编程，探索 STEM。我们致力于在全球范围内促进青少年的科学、技术与创新实践。 Beyond computer science coding. We promote and facilitate STEM and innovation for youth in Canada and worldwide.

我们的使命Our Objectives

通过互联网、媒体和各类活动，在加拿大及全球范围内推广 STEM 教育，鼓励青少年参与科学技术讨论与社交。 To promote STEM beyond computer coding through internet, media, and activities, supporting youth in Canada and worldwide.

创新实践Innovative Practice

我们强调负责任的实践，关注信息共享与讨论，帮助孩子在创新的道路上走得更远。 We focus on information sharing, networking, and accountable practice for youth in science and technology innovation.
